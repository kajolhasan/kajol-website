About My Journey

Welcome to my vibrant and captivating art world. I’m a **Bangladeshi** artist living in **Narayanganj, Bangladesh**. My art rises from the search for self-definition and understanding the essence of life itself. Through striking, colorful, and clear visual language my work captures the collective human experience and reveals its universality through personal narrative exploration.
